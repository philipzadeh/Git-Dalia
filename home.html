MISSION & VISION

"DALIA - Data literacy
for all from the very
beginning!"

DALIA promotes the learning and teaching of Data Literacy through the curated provision of open teaching and learning content, empowering everyone to collect, analyze, critically evaluate, and use data in a FAIR way.

Our Connection

We are part of NFDI e.V.

DALIA is closely connected to the NFDI e.V. and creates active synergies with the NFDI sections Training and Education (EduTrain) and Ethical, Legal, and Social aspects (ELSA) but also Metadata, Terminology, and Provenance and Infra. The project makes an important contribution to increasing data literacy and establishing a data culture in the academic environment and beyond.
