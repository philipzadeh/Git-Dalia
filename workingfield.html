Quality Control and Continuation

Generic and domain specific quality standards for learning and teaching material, metadata standards and mechanisms are the workingfields main aims. To not develop just another standard a close coorporations is held with the NFDI Consortia and the NFDI Sections “Education and Training”, Metadata and „Ethical, Legal and Social Aspects, „(Meta)data, Terminology and Provenance“.

Work Package 01

Provider community & integration in NFDI section

Communication and networking with the respective communities, stakeholders and other projects and initiatives (national and international) is essential in order to obtain all relevant input for the knowledge base. As the project is linked to a large number of existing national and international projects and initiatives, dedicated coordination is intended. Above all, the materials from the NFDI section "Training & Education" will be integrated into the platform during the course of the work package. A direct feedback loop will be established between the section and the knowledge base. Successfully anchoring the knowledge base in the section will ensure the sustainability of the service.
